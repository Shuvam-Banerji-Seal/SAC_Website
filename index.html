Twelve Clubs, One Campus, Endless Reasons To Show Up

The Student Activity Council is the umbrella body that gathers every cultural and academic society under one editorial roof. This edition is the index — turn the page to find your section.

By the Editorial Desk · SAC Communications

The Student Activity Council (SAC) at IISER Kolkata curates the year-round calendar of cultural and academic life on campus. It coordinates ten cultural clubs, two standing committees, and the institute's contingent for the Inter-IISER Cultural Meet. This page is the printed directory of that ecosystem — laid out by body, with each section unfolding as you scroll.

Inside, every society is treated as its own article. The cards that follow carry each club's logo, name, and a short excerpt drawn from the official club information document — the same text you'd find on the individual club page, only here it is set smaller, beside the fold. Click any card to read the full feature.

Where a body has no entries yet (Sports, for example), the page leaves the column open rather than invent material that is not in our records. New editions of the Chronicle will arrive with the new tenure's office-bearer portraits and the year's highlights.
