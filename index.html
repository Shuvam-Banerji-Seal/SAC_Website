A Campus In Print: The Work And Life Of Twelve Societies

The Student Activity Council brings together every cultural, academic, and residential society at IISER Kolkata under a single administrative body. This edition is a full directory — each club profiled in its own article, each section a chapter in the year's story.

From the Editorial Desk · SAC Communications

The Student Activity Council (SAC) at IISER Kolkata oversees the year-round calendar of cultural and academic life on campus. It coordinates ten cultural clubs, sixteen sports clubs, two academic committees, and a hostel committee committees, and the institute's contingent for the Inter-IISER Cultural Meet — a broad ecosystem of performance, debate, inquiry, and expression that runs from the first week of the academic session to the last.

Every society below is represented on a card bearing its logo, name, and a short introduction drawn from the club's official records. These excerpts are the same opening paragraphs you will find on the club's dedicated page: a consistent voice across the entire edition. Click any card to read the full profile, browse the office-bearer gallery, and explore the year's events.

Where a body has no entries yet — Sports, for instance — the space is left open rather than filled with placeholder material. New editions of the Chronicle will arrive with the incoming tenure's office-bearer portraits, competition results, and the highlights of each club's season. This is a living document, printed in code, and updated as the year unfolds.
