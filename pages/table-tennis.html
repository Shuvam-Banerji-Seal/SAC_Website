Table Tennis Club

The Table Tennis Club of IISER Kolkata is a fast-paced community of ping-pong enthusiasts. From casual rallies during breaks to intense competitive matches, the club offers an exciting outlet for quick reflexes and strategic play.

With regular practice sessions, coaching from experienced players, and inter-batch tournaments, the club is the go-to place for anyone looking to pick up a paddle and join the action.

Events & Activities

The Table Tennis Club organises regular practice sessions, intra-campus tournaments, and selection trials for representing IISER Kolkata at IICM and other inter-collegiate table tennis competitions.

Achievements

The club regularly participates in Inter-IISER Cultural Meet (IICM) sports events and other inter-collegiate table tennis tournaments, bringing laurels to IISER Kolkata.
