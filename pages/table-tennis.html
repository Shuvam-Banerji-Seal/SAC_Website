Table Tennis Club

The IISER Kolkata Table Tennis Club is a student-run sports club dedicated to promoting table tennis and a vibrant sporting culture on campus. The club provides students with opportunities to learn, practice, and compete through regular coaching sessions, encouraging teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship.

Table tennis is a fast-paced sport that combines physical fitness, mental focus, quick reflexes, and strategic thinking. Over the years, the club has successfully organized several tournaments and represented IISER Kolkata in inter-college and inter-IISER Sports Meets.

Events & Activities

Major events include Practice Sessions, Fresher's Tournament, Inter Batch Tournament (highest participation), Spring Pong (auction-style tournament), IISM Selection Tournament, and IISM Practice Sessions. The club also organized an inter-college tournament as part of Inquivesta XII.

Achievements

The club has successfully organized several tournaments over the years and represented IISER Kolkata in inter-college and inter-IISER Sports Meets, building a strong and inclusive table tennis community on campus.
