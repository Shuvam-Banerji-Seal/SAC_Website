Volleyball Club

VOLIK (Volleyball Club of IISER Kolkata) stands as one of the most engaging and widely participated communities on campus, committed to building a larger, inclusive volleyball family where the joy of every spike and the energy on the court serve as a powerful recharge from demanding academic schedules.

The club achieved a silver medal at the 2025 IISM (Inter IISER Sports Meet) and is witnessing a surge in women's participation. VOLIK is supported by a formidable alumni network known as "VOLIK-Star" that provides mentorship and financial assistance.

Events & Activities

Events include National Sports Day celebrations, Freshers' Match, Interbatch Tournament (Boys & Girls), Super Smash League (SSL), Women's Super Smash League (WSSL), All-Stars Match, and IISM training and participation throughout the year.

Achievements

VOLIK achieved a silver medal at the 2025 IISM (Inter IISER Sports Meet), proving the competitive strength of its players. The club continues to foster a vibrant volleyball culture with growing participation, especially among women athletes on campus.
