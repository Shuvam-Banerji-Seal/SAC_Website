Singularity — The Astronomy Club

Founded in 2023 by a group of passionate astrophiles, Singularity is the Astronomy Club of IISER Kolkata. What began as a small community of stargazers has grown into one of the most active and intellectually vibrant clubs on campus, dedicated to making the wonders of the universe accessible to every curious mind.

Activities

  • Stargazing Sessions: Regular night-sky observation events on campus, equipped with telescopes and guided by experienced members.
  • Student Talks: Weekly or bi-weekly talks where students present topics ranging from stellar evolution to cosmology.
  • Scientist Interviews: Interactions and interviews with professional astronomers and researchers.
  • Workshops: Hands-on sessions on telescope handling, astrophotography, data analysis, and sky-charting software.
  • Projects: Collaborative research and observation projects undertaken by members throughout the academic year.
  • Quizzes & Discussions: Astronomy quizzes, journal clubs, and open discussion forums to foster scientific curiosity.

ISAAC — Indian Synergy of Astronomy and Astrophysics Clubs

Singularity founded ISAAC (Indian Synergy of Astronomy and Astrophysics Clubs), a nationwide network that connects astronomy clubs across Indian institutions. ISAAC provides a collaborative platform for sharing resources, organizing joint events, and building a unified community of amateur and professional astronomers across the country.
