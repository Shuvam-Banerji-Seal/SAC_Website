Badminton Club

The Badminton Club of IISER Kolkata is a vibrant community of shuttle enthusiasts who take to the court with energy and enthusiasm. From casual rally sessions to intense competitive matches, the club caters to players of all skill levels.

With regular practice sessions and inter-batch tournaments, the club nurtures talent and sportsmanship while providing a fun outlet for physical activity on campus.

Events & Activities

The Badminton Club hosts regular practice sessions, inter-batch tournaments, and selection trials for IICM and other inter-collegiate competitions throughout the year.

Achievements

The club regularly participates in Inter-IISER Cultural Meet (IICM) sports events and other inter-collegiate badminton tournaments, bringing laurels to IISER Kolkata.
