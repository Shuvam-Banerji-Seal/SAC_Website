SAC Hostel Committee

The Students Hostel Committee is the backbone of residential life at IISER Kolkata. From maintenance and safety to community-building and grievance redressal, the committee ensures that every student’s stay on campus is comfortable, secure, and enriching.

Structure & Rules

The Hostel Committee follows a well-defined hierarchical structure to ensure efficient governance across all hostels and wings.

  • Each floor of every wing has one Wing Representative.
  • Each sub-committee comprises 5 members: 1 Convenor, 1 Associate Convenor, and 3 Coordinators.
  • Hierarchy: Convenor > Associate Convenor > Coordinators.

Sub-Committees

The Hostel Committee operates through the following sub-committees, each tasked with a specific domain of hostel welfare:

  • Health and Hygiene
  • Safety and Security
  • Repair and Maintenance
  • Purchase and Handling
  • Communication and Grievances

Hostel Wings

IISER Kolkata’s residential campus is organised into several hostel wings, each with multiple blocks and floors. Every floor is represented by a Wing Representative elected from among its residents.
