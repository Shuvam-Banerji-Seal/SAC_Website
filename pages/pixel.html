PIXEL — Photography Club of IISER Kolkata

PIXEL is the Photography Club of IISER Kolkata, a community of phone and DSLR photographers united by a shared passion for visual storytelling. We are not just here to take pictures — we are here to learn how to truly see.

From the fleeting golden hour over the campus fields to the quiet intensity of a late-night studio session, PIXEL finds meaning in everyday moments. We believe every frame is an opportunity to discover something new about the world and ourselves.

Whether you shoot on your phone during a walk or carry a full-frame camera on a photowalk, PIXEL provides the community, the mentorship, and the platform to grow as a photographer and as a keen observer of life.

Events

PIXEL organizes a wide range of photographic events throughout the year, from photowalks to inter-college competitions, giving members ample opportunity to sharpen their skills and showcase their work.

Photowalks

The club organizes 3 to 4 photowalks per year, covering diverse locations around Kolkata and beyond. These walks bring members together to explore, observe, and capture the world through their lenses while learning from each other’s perspectives and techniques.

PHOTON — at Inquivesta

PHOTON is PIXEL’s flagship event held during Inquivesta, the annual science fest of IISER Kolkata. It features competitive photography rounds that test participants’ technical skill, creative vision, and ability to tell a story through images.

Shuttercycles

A weekly themed photography challenge where members are given a prompt and a timeframe to shoot and share their best frame. Shuttercycles keep the creative juices flowing throughout the semester and build a vibrant culture of consistent practice and peer feedback.

Equipment Rules

  1. Equipment is available only for club members with valid membership.
  2. Items must be borrowed and returned on the same day unless otherwise approved by the OB.
  3. A valid college ID must be submitted as collateral when borrowing any item.
  4. Handle all equipment with care. Any damage must be reported immediately to the OB.
  5. Do not lend club equipment to non-members or outside individuals under any circumstances.
  6. Equipment requests must be made at least 24 hours in advance via the club email.
  7. All stored data on SD cards must be copied and deleted before returning the card.
  8. Violation of any rule may result in temporary suspension of borrowing privileges.
