Kabaddi Club

The IISER Kolkata Kabaddi Club is a passionate and energetic sports community dedicated to promoting the spirit of kabaddi among students. The club brings together players who share enthusiasm for teamwork, fitness, discipline, and the traditional essence of Indian sports, creating a competitive yet supportive environment where both beginners and experienced players can develop their skills and confidence.

The club regularly organizes practice sessions, friendly matches, inter-batch competitions, and has proudly represented IISER Kolkata in events such as IISM and Parakram at IIT Dhanbad, delivering impactful performances and showcasing exceptional teamwork and sportsmanship. Our mission is to inspire students to stay active, embrace challenges, and contribute to the growing kabaddi culture within the institute.

With determination, discipline, and team spirit, the IISER Kolkata Kabaddi Club continues to grow as a strong sporting community that celebrates passion, perseverance, and excellence.

Events & Activities

The club organizes regular practice sessions, friendly matches, and inter-batch competitions. Beyond the game, the club focuses on building unity, leadership, mental strength, and camaraderie among members. Kabaddi is not just a sport — it is a symbol of strategy, resilience, and teamwork.

Achievements

The team has proudly represented IISER Kolkata at IISM and Parakram at IIT Dhanbad, showcasing exceptional teamwork, determination, and sportsmanship on a competitive stage.
