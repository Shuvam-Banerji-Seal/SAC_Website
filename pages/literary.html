Literary Club — IISER Kolkata

The Literary Club of IISER Kolkata is a space for curiosity, creativity and conversation. It brings together readers, writers, poets, debaters, and anyone with a love for the written and spoken word. The club provides a platform for students to express themselves through prose, poetry, dramatics, and intellectual discourse.

Throughout the year, the club organises a wide range of events — from literary festivals and storytelling competitions to puzzle hunts and creative writing workshops. It is a community where ideas flow freely and every voice finds a stage.

Beyond events, the Literary Club nurtures a culture of reading, thinking, and articulating ideas. Members engage in regular reading circles, discussion sessions, and collaborative projects that celebrate the richness of literature in all its forms.

Events

The Literary Club’s calendar is packed with events that celebrate the written and spoken word, from grand annual festivals to regular weekly activities.

Lexis

Lexis is the annual literary festival of IISER Kolkata, organised entirely by the Literary Club. It is one of the most anticipated events on the institute’s cultural calendar, featuring inter-college competitions in debate, creative writing, quiz, dramatics, and more. The festival brings together participants from colleges across the region for a multi-day celebration of literature and intellect.

Week of the Fiction

Week of the Fiction is a dedicated week-long celebration of storytelling and reading. The event features reading circles, book discussions, fiction writing prompts, and interactive sessions that encourage students to dive into the world of narrative and imagination.

Bi-Weekly Puzzles

The club releases bi-weekly puzzles covering a range of formats — from crosswords and word searches to logic puzzles and cryptic challenges. These regular instalments keep members and the wider campus engaged with creative problem-solving throughout the year.
