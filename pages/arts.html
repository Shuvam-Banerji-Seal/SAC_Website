Arts Club — IISER Kolkata

Arts Club of IISER Kolkata is undoubtedly the ultimate powerhouse for creativity in the campus, where all forms of imagination and visual expression are celebrated under one roof. From painting and sketching to sculpting, crafting, and mixed-media experimentation, the club provides a vibrant space for students to explore and hone their artistic abilities regardless of prior experience.

The club thrives on the belief that art is not limited to a canvas — it lives in every idea, every colour, and every act of creation. Members are encouraged to experiment freely, collaborate across disciplines, and push the boundaries of visual storytelling. Workshops, exhibitions, and interactive sessions form the backbone of the club’s activities throughout the year.

Beyond the creative pursuits, the Arts Club fosters a close-knit community bound by mutual appreciation for aesthetics and expression. It is a space where beginners find encouragement, seasoned artists find stimulation, and everyone finds a home for their imagination.

Events

The Arts Club organises a diverse range of events throughout the academic year, from large-scale exhibitions to immersive themed gatherings that bring the campus together through creativity.

Chitrakavyam

Chitrakavyam is the club’s flagship event — an exhibition-cum-auction of artworks created by the members and the broader campus community. The event provides a platform for artists to showcase their work, engage with art enthusiasts, and see their creations find new homes through a lively auction process. It celebrates the intersection of art, community, and expression.

Halloween

The Arts Club’s annual Halloween celebration transforms the campus into a festive spectacle featuring a costume party, an immersive escape room, and a DJ night. The event blends creative costume design with interactive entertainment, drawing participation from across batches and departments.
