Basketball Club

ISCOBAC (IISER Kolkata Basketball Club) is the official basketball club of IISER Kolkata, dedicated to promoting the sport and fostering a vibrant sporting culture within the institute. The club serves as a platform for students to develop their athletic abilities, teamwork, leadership skills, and sportsmanship while representing IISER Kolkata in various inter-college and inter-IISER competitions.

The club provides regular training and skill-development opportunities for players of all experience levels, and organizes intra-campus competitions, friendly matches, and recruitment drives for new students.

Club Objectives

  1. Promote basketball as a sport on campus
  2. Provide regular training and practice sessions
  3. Foster physical fitness, discipline, and teamwork
  4. Build a competitive team for IISM and other tournaments
  5. Create an inclusive environment for players of all skill levels

Events & Activities

Events include the Fresher Match, IISM Practice Sessions, Practice Matches with Other Colleges, IISM participation, Interbatch Matches, Inquesta 3v3, All-Star Matches, Auction Tournament, and Farewell Matches. Club activities also include court maintenance, equipment management, and coordinating with the Sports Office.

Achievements

ISCOBAC has achieved consistent participation in Inter-IISER Sports Meets and various inter-college basketball tournaments, developing a strong basketball community and fostering student participation and athletic excellence on campus. A well trained basketball team under a great coach is what we have as our power.
