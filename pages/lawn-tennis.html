Lawn Tennis Club

IKTC (IISER Kolkata Tennis Club) encourages students, staff, and professors to come together for a fresh breath of air from the relentless academic world. The club motivates and fosters growth of tennis among the community, helping develop a calculative and strategic mind as well as teamwork and leadership qualities.

Everyone, from advanced players to absolute beginners, is highly encouraged to engage in the activities of the club and contribute to its growth. IKTC has always made a mark at IISM events, continuously bringing in gold and silver medals across categories.

Events & Activities

Yearly activities include Freshers' Tournament, Open Tournament for IISM selection, Inter-batch Tournament (all three formats), introductory sessions by the OBs, and practice sessions.

How to Begin

Racquets and balls are available with the OBs of the club. If you require them for practice, please contact the OBs. Talks for a more systematic procurement of racquets is underway.

Booking of courts is done in the WhatsApp group of IKTC according to the rules provided there.

Beginners are encouraged to ask the seniors for help!
