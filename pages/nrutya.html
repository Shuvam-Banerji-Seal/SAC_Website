Nrutya — The Dance Club of IISER Kolkata

Nrutya, the Dance Club of IISER Kolkata, celebrates the joy and power of dance as a universal form of expression. The club provides an inclusive space for students of all skill levels — from beginners taking their first steps to experienced dancers honing their craft.

Through workshops, practice sessions, and performances, Nrutya fosters a vibrant dance culture on campus. Members explore a wide range of dance forms including semi-classical, hip-hop, contemporary, folk, and freestyle, creating a dynamic environment where creativity and passion thrive.

Beyond regular practice sessions, the club organizes major events that bring the campus together in celebration of dance. Whether it is the festive Garba Night or the high-energy Interbatch Dance Competition, Nrutya ensures there is always a stage for every dancer.

Events

Nrutya hosts a vibrant calendar of events throughout the academic year, offering something for every dance enthusiast on campus.

Garba Night

Held annually in October during Navratri, the Garba Night is one of the most anticipated events on the IISER Kolkata campus. Open to all students with a nominal entry fee, the evening brings together students, faculty, and staff in a spirited celebration of traditional Gujarati dance, music, and festivity, with food and beverage stalls adding to the atmosphere. Introduced in 2024, the event is a cultural highlight that unites the entire campus community.

Nrutyang — Mega Workshops

Nrutyang is a series of intensive dance workshops organized by the club throughout the year. The workshops include a 4-day Semi-Classical Workshop that introduces participants to the grace and technique of Indian semi-classical dance, and a Hip-Hop Workshop that covers contemporary hip-hop styles and choreography — with 25+ participants, it is one of the largest and most successful workshops organized by Nrutya. These workshops are open to students of all skill levels.

Interbatch Dance Competition

The Interbatch Dance Competition is the flagship competitive event of Nrutya, bringing together teams from different batches to compete in a spectacular showcase of dance talent. Judged by a distinguished panel of renowned national level dancers, the competition features multiple categories including solo, duet, and group performances across various dance styles.

Rules & Regulations

Room Booking

Dance room bookings are to be made via the Nrutya WhatsApp group. The maximum booking duration is 2 hours. Members should confirm their booking through the designated OB before using the room.

Electronics Handling

The club’s Bluetooth speaker and other electronic equipment must be handled with care. Members should not adjust volume settings beyond recommended levels. Any malfunction or damage must be reported to the OBs immediately.

Cleanliness

The dance room must be kept clean and tidy at all times. Members are expected to wipe the floor mats after use, ensure all personal belongings are taken, and leave the room in the same condition as found.

Club Assets

  • Makeup kit
  • Knee caps
