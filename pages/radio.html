Campus Radio — IISER KOLKATA (IKCR)

Campus Radio IISER Kolkata (IKCR) is the definitive voice of the student community. By the students, for the students — we exist to bridge the gap between what the campus thinks and what the campus hears.

IKCR is built on the principle of being unfiltered, unbiased, and student-first. From academic discussions to cultural commentary, from music to mental health, we provide a platform for every voice that deserves to be heard on campus.

Whether it is live coverage of a cultural fest, a late-night radio segment on student life, or a podcast dissecting current affairs, IKCR brings the campus community closer together through the power of audio and storytelling.

Events

Campus Radio IISER Kolkata covers and organizes a wide spectrum of events throughout the academic year, from cultural celebrations to live sports coverage and academic orientations.

Smarane Rabindranath

A tribute to the literary and musical legacy of Rabindranath Tagore, this event features readings, performances, and discussions celebrating the timeless works of the Nobel laureate, broadcast live across campus.

Mahishasur Mardini

IKCR covers the grand celebration of Mahishasur Mardini, capturing the devotional music, performances, and cultural significance of this cherished annual tradition at IISER Kolkata.

Parakram Divas

A day dedicated to valour and remembrance, IKCR brings live coverage and reflective segments honouring the spirit of courage and sacrifice on Parakram Divas.

Cupidology

A fun-filled Valentine’s special event organized by IKCR, featuring live dedications, interactive segments, and a whole lot of campus romance broadcast over the airwaves.

Sports Coverage & Cultural Events

From live commentary during intra-campus sports tournaments to behind-the-scenes coverage of cultural fests, IKCR ensures that every major campus moment is captured and shared with the entire student body.

Orientations

IKCR plays a key role during freshman orientations, producing welcome segments, campus guides, and introductory content that helps new students settle into life at IISER Kolkata.

IICM Achievements

In 2023, IKCR won the Gold Medal in Bandwidth at the Inter-IISER Cultural Meet — a testament to the club's creative and technical excellence.
