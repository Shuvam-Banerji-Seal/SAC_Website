GYM Club

The gym club of IISER Kolkata promotes fitness, health, and an active lifestyle among students and faculty alike. It provides a space for strength training, powerlifting, bodybuilding, and general physical fitness. The club organizes workout sessions, fitness-related events, and supports participation in sports and fitness initiatives on campus.

With access to the institute's sports complex and gym facilities, the club aims to create a motivating environment where students can improve both physical and mental health, de-stress from academic pressure, and build a strong sense of community through fitness and teamwork.
