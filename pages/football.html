Football Club

The Football Club of IISER Kolkata (FCIK) is dedicated to the continuous improvement of the footballing culture at IISER Kolkata. The club serves every student with a passion for the game, be it through playing or watching the sport. FCIK is responsible for ground maintenance, match screenings, and proper maintenance of equipment so that everyone who wants to fall in love with the beautiful game can do so.

The club promotes football and its health benefits among everyone, from seasoned players to beginners, and trains both boys' and girls' teams to represent the institute at various tournaments, most importantly the Inter-IISER Sports Meet (IISM).

Events & Activities

Activities include regular coaching sessions for boys and girls, practice matches, inter-batch and auction tournaments, freshers' and farewell matches, screening of important football matches, and ground and equipment maintenance.

Achievements

Boys' team: Champions at IISM 2012, 2014, and 2016; Runners-up at IISM 2013 and 2015; fourth place at IISM 2023 and 2025. Girls' team: Champions at IISM 2019 and 2025; Runners-up at IISM 2016; 2nd Runners-up at IISM 2024. Both teams are regular participants at IISM.
