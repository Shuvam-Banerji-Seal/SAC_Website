Athletics Club

ACIK (Athletics Club of IISER Kolkata) actively engages in a wide range of track and field events, including sprints (100m, 200m, 400m), middle and long-distance races (800m to 10000m), as well as field events such as shot put, discus throw, javelin throw, long jump, triple jump, and relays.

The club proudly represents IISER Kolkata in major competitions such as Pratap (Inter-College Athletics Meet), Open Athletics Meets, and IISM (Inter IISER-NISER-CEBS Sports Meet). Alongside these, ACIK organizes internal competitions like Inter-Batch Meets and Freshers events, encouraging participation and healthy competition on campus.

Events & Activities

Major events include Pratap (Battle of Athletics), an inter-college meet bringing together students from different colleges; the Inter-Batch Athletics Event; Fresher's Athletics Events; Freedom Run and Ganrajyam Pride Run (5 km campus-wide runs); the Open IISER Sprint, Throw, and Jump Meets open to all campus members; and IISM, the annual inter-institutional sporting event featuring track and field disciplines.

Achievements

ACIK regularly represents IISER Kolkata at IISM and other inter-college athletics meets, consistently bringing laurels to the institute through competitive performances across track and field disciplines.
