IKQC — Quiz Club of IISER Kolkata

IKQC, the quiz club of IISER Kolkata, is a dynamic community of curious minds united by a passion for knowledge, trivia, and the thrill of competition. From general knowledge to science, history, pop culture, and beyond, IKQC brings together students who love to question, learn, and test their wits.

The club organises regular quiz sessions, workshops, and inter-college competitions that challenge participants across a wide spectrum of topics. Whether it is a quick-fire round or a meticulously crafted set, every event is designed to be both intellectually stimulating and enjoyable.

IKQC has built a reputation for excellence at IICM and various inter-college festivals, consistently bringing home trophies and recognition. Beyond competitions, the club fosters a welcoming environment where newcomers can learn the art of quiz creation and become confident quizzers themselves.

Events

IKQC hosts a range of quiz events throughout the year, from welcoming newcomers to representing IISER Kolkata at prestigious inter-college competitions.

Freshers’ Quiz

The Freshers’ Quiz is the club’s introductory event for incoming students. It serves as an engaging entry point into the world of quizzing, covering a mix of general knowledge, science, pop culture, and campus life. The event helps new students discover the club and connect with fellow quiz enthusiasts.

Dublin Wager

Dublin Wager is IKQC’s flagship inter-college quiz competition, hosted as part of Lexis, the annual literary festival of IISER Kolkata. The event attracts teams from colleges across the region, competing in a multi-round quiz that spans diverse topics and formats. It is one of the most anticipated events at Lexis each year.

Inquizzitive

Inquizzitive is a general quiz competition held during Inquivesta, the annual science festival of IISER Kolkata. The quiz tests participants on a broad spectrum of topics and draws enthusiastic participation from across the institute and beyond.
