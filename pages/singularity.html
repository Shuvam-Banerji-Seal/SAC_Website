Singularity — The Astronomy Club

Singularity is the official Astronomy club of IISER Kolkata, founded in 2023 by a group of passionate astrophiles. Since then, it has consistently been one of the most active clubs in the institute, with equally active and passionate members.

The club aims to foster passion and genuine interest in astronomy through stargazing sessions, student talks, scientist interviews, workshops, projects, quizzes, and discussions. Singularity has also founded ISAAC (Indian Synergy of Astronomy and Astrophysics Clubs), a student-led nationwide network for astronomy clubs to collaborate and share learning and resources.
