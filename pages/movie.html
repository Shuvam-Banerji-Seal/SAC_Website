Movie Club — IISER K

Movie Club of IISER Kolkata is dedicated to providing exquisite taste in movies to the campus community. We stream, we craft, we develop films — and in doing so, we bring people from different backgrounds together under the banner of a shared love for cinema.

Whether you are a cinephile who can recite entire screenplays or someone who just enjoys a good story on a lazy weekend, Movie Club welcomes you. Our screenings span genres, languages, and eras — from timeless classics to contemporary independent cinema — ensuring there is always something for everyone.

Beyond screenings, the club actively engages members in filmmaking, from scriptwriting and storyboarding to shooting and post-production. It is a space where creativity meets collaboration, and where every frame tells a story worth sharing.

Events

Movie Club organizes a range of film-centric events throughout the year, from curated screenings to hands-on filmmaking competitions, keeping the campus engaged with the art of cinema.

Visit to KIFF

One of the most anticipated outings of the year, members of Movie Club get the opportunity to attend screenings and events at the Kolkata International Film Festival, exposing them to global cinema, industry discussions, and the vibrant culture of a major film festival.

Interbatch Filmmaking Competition

An annual showcase of campus talent, this competition invites teams from different batches to produce short films on a given theme. From concept to final cut, it is a complete filmmaking experience that brings out the best storytellers on campus.

Regular Weekend Screenings

On most weekends, the club hosts curated movie screenings for members and non-members alike. These events range from themed marathon sessions to single-film discussions, always accompanied by lively conversations about the craft of filmmaking and the stories told on screen.

Achievements

IICM Achievements

Movie Club has represented IISER Kolkata at the Indian Inter-College Meet with pride. Notably, the club secured a Bronze Medal at IICM 2024 with the short film “Orphaned”, a powerful piece that resonated with audiences and judges alike.
