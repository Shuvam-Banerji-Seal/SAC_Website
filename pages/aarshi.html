AARSHI — The Drama Club

AARSHI, the Theatrical and Dramatics Society of IISER Kolkata, is a vibrant collective of storytellers, performers, writers, directors, and theatre enthusiasts united by a shared passion for the performing arts.

It envisions to promote classical theatre acting and various forms of acting amongst its members. It intends to amalgamate cultures from various parts of the country and provide them a platform to showcase the rich heritage of their state on stage.

Beyond the stage lights and performances, AARSHI stands as a family — a space built on trust, collaboration, learning, and shared memories. It encourages confidence, teamwork, discipline, and artistic freedom, nurturing individuals both as performers and as people. With every production, AARSHI seeks to inspire, provoke thought, and celebrate the timeless magic of theatre.

Events

AARSHI remains active throughout the academic year with a vibrant lineup of theatrical productions, competitions, workshops and creative events that foster artistic expression and strengthen the theatre culture within IISER Kolkata.

Mrignayanee

One of the flagship events of the society is Mrignayanee, the annual Online Summer Reel-Making Competition that encourages participants to explore storytelling, acting, editing, and cinematic creativity through short-form digital content. The event provides students with a platform to experiment with performance beyond the stage and showcase their creative vision.

Annual Drama Workshop

Another major initiative of AARSHI is the Annual Drama Workshop and Production, where members undergo intensive training in acting, stage movement, voice modulation, improvisation, and production techniques under experienced mentors. The workshop culminates in a full-fledged theatrical production staged before a live audience. Over the years, the society has presented notable productions such as Sab Changa Si (2022), Andh-Dwand (2023), PankhJhat (2024), and Lakeer-e-Kabaddi (2025), each reflecting unique narratives and diverse theatrical styles.

Rangabhumi & Abhivyakti

AARSHI also organizes Rangabhumi, the Inter-Batch Drama Competition of IISER Kolkata. The event brings together students from different batches to conceptualize, write, direct, and perform original theatrical productions, fostering teamwork, creativity, and healthy competition within the campus community. Another prominent event hosted by the society is Abhivyakti, the Monodrama Competition of IISER Kolkata. The competition celebrates the art of solo performance and serves as the selection platform for the institute’s representative for the IICM Monodrama Competition.

Achievements

IICM Achievements

AARSHI has consistently brought laurels to IISER Kolkata through its remarkable achievements in IICM. The society secured Gold in Group Play at the IICM theatre competitions for three consecutive years — 2023, 2024, and 2025 — reflecting its excellence in acting, direction, production, and stagecraft.

The society has also achieved outstanding success in the Monodrama category. Sharanya Chatterjee secured Gold in Monodrama consecutively in 2024 and 2025, further establishing AARSHI’s strong presence in competitive theatre circuits among IISERs. Notably, AARSHI was entitled the “THESPIAN TITANS” in IICM 2024 for excellence in drama among all IISERs.
