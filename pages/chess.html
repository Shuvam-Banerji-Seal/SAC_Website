Chess Club

IKCC (Chess Club of IISER Kolkata) is a vibrant community of students brought together by their passion for chess. The club provides a platform for players of all skill levels to learn, practice, and compete in a friendly and intellectually stimulating environment through regular casual games, training sessions, puzzles, and tournaments.

Whether you are a seasoned player or a beginner eager to learn, the Chess Club welcomes everyone to explore the endless possibilities of the 64 squares while fostering strategic thinking, sportsmanship, and camaraderie.

Club Objectives

  1. Promote chess as a strategic sport on campus
  2. Provide learning opportunities for beginners and advanced players
  3. Foster critical thinking, strategic planning, and sportsmanship
  4. Organize tournaments, workshops, and practice sessions
  5. Encourage competitive participation in IISM and other events
  6. Build an inclusive community of chess enthusiasts
  7. Represent IISER Kolkata in inter-institutional competitions

Events & Activities

The club organizes Inter-Batch Chess Tournaments, the Open Chess Tournament during Inquivesta, and numerous tournaments featuring Classical, Rapid, Blitz, and other innovative time control formats. IKCC also conducts introductory sessions and recruitment drives for new students.

Major Activities

  • Practice sessions and coaching programs for club members
  • Team selection and training for inter-college and inter-IISER tournaments
  • Intra-campus chess competitions and friendly matches
  • Introductory sessions and recruitment drives for new students
  • Sports fest participation, invitational tournaments, and institute-level events
  • Workshops, exhibitions, and community engagement activities

Achievements

The club has achieved podium finishes at the Inter IISER Sports Meet (IISM) for three consecutive years. IKCC regularly organizes Inter-Batch Chess Tournaments and successfully conducts the Open Chess Tournament during Inquivesta, attracting participants from within and outside the institute.
