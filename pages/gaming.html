Gaming Club

GCIK (Gaming Club of IISER Kolkata) is the official gaming club that brings together gaming enthusiasts on campus. The club provides a platform for students to connect through video games, esports, and other interactive gaming activities, fostering both recreation and community building.

Known for organising gaming nights, tournaments, and multiplayer gaming events, GCIK promotes teamwork, strategic thinking, and healthy competition while creating a fun and inclusive environment for students to unwind and connect with their peers.

Events & Activities

Events include the Freshers Tournament, IIESM Selection Matches, Inter-batch Tournament, Multi-sports Auction Tournament, Farewell Tournament, and Casual Competitions and Community Nights. Titles played include Free Fire, Valorant, BGMI, Call of Duty, CS2, Marvel Rivals, Deadshot, eFootball, and more.

Achievements

GCIK successfully organized regular gaming nights, LAN events, and inter-hostel tournaments. The club played a key role in organising Headshot, the flagship esports event of Inquivesta, and represented IISER Kolkata in inter-institute esports competitions.
