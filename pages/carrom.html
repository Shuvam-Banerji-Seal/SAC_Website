Carrom Club

CCIK (Carrom Club of IISER Kolkata) is a student-run club that brings together carrom enthusiasts across the campus. It provides a platform for students to learn, practice, and compete in the game while promoting sportsmanship, strategic thinking, and community engagement through regular sessions and tournaments.

The club represents IISER Kolkata at IISM and other competitions, and organizes Freshers' Tournaments, Inter-Batch Tournaments, Strike Tournaments, and Auction Tournaments throughout the academic year.

Club Objectives

  1. Promote carrom as a recreational and competitive sport on campus
  2. Provide a platform for students to learn and practice carrom
  3. Organize regular matches, tournaments, and practice sessions
  4. Encourage sportsmanship, strategic thinking, and social interaction
  5. Foster social interaction and community building through the sport
  6. Represent IISER Kolkata in IISM and other inter-institutional tournaments

Events & Activities

Events include the Fresher Match, IISM Practice Sessions, Practice Matches, IISM participation, Interbatch Matches, Auction Tournament, and Strike Tournament. The club also organizes regular carrom tournaments, hosts Inter-Batch Championships, and trains students for inter-institute competitions like IISM.

Achievements

The IISER Kolkata Carrom Team secured a Bronze Medal at the Inter IISER Sports Meet (IISM-2025). The club has significantly increased student participation and fostered a competitive yet inclusive sporting culture on campus.
