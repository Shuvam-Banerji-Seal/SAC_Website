Rubik's Cube Club

Established in 2018, the Rubik's Cube Club of IISER Kolkata was born out of a shared passion to promote Speedcubing — the art of speed-solving the Rubik's Cube and other twisty puzzles. The club fosters a vibrant cubing culture not only among IISER Kolkata students but also across colleges throughout West Bengal and India.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, the club created AICS (All IISER Cubing Society) to keep the community connected across all IISERs, NISER, and IISc through online events and virtual competitions. The club has since revived with WCA tournaments and continues to grow.

Events & Activities

Flagship events include Freshers and Interbatch Tournaments, Inquivesta Cube Open (an official WCA event), and the IISERK Spring and Autumn Cube Opens — signature seasonal WCA tournaments conducted each semester.

Achievements

The club regularly organizes official WCA tournaments, hosts the Inquivesta Cube Open, and conducts seasonal WCA competitions, bringing recognition to IISER Kolkata in the national and international cubing community.
