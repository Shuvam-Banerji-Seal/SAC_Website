Kho-Kho Club

The Kho-Kho Club of IISER Kolkata (KCIK) is a vibrant, student-run sports community dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of the traditional Indian sport. With a strong focus on regular training, strategic gameplay, and rigorous conditioning, KCIK actively nurtures talent and fosters a passion for the sport within the campus.

KCIK boasts a dedicated and well-maintained training facility at the IISER Kolkata Kho-Kho Ground in the Haringhata Farm area, illuminated for regular practice sessions, inter-batch games, and official tournaments hosted by the club.

Events & Activities

Events for 2026-27 include All Stars Match (26 September), IISM Practice Matches (October-December), Fresher's Match (13 November), Interbatch Tournament (February-March), Auction Tournament (11-19 March), and Farewell Match (10 April).

Achievements

The KCIK men's squad clinched the Gold Medal at the Inter-IISER Sports Meet (IISM), breaking an 11-year drought for the men's team. KCIK also takes pride in its expanding national footprint, actively training and supporting both men's and women's contingents to participate in various national-level Kho-Kho events.
