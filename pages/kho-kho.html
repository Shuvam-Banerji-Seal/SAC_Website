Kho-Kho Club

The Kho-Kho Club of IISER Kolkata (KCIK) is a vibrant, student-run sports community dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of the traditional Indian sport. With a strong focus on regular training, strategic gameplay, and rigorous conditioning, KCIK actively nurtures talent and fosters a passion for the sport within the campus.

KCIK boasts a dedicated and well-maintained training facility at the IISER Kolkata Kho-Kho Ground in the Haringhata Farm area, illuminated for regular practice sessions, inter-batch games, and official tournaments hosted by the club.

Events & Activities

The Kho-Kho Club of IISER Kolkata is dedicated to promoting the sport through a comprehensive annual plan designed to foster skill development, competitive spirit, and a strong sense of community among its members. Our activities cater to all levels of experience, from welcoming new students to celebrating our graduating athletes.

Fresher's Match

A warm welcome for new students, introducing them to the fundamentals of kho-kho, building essential chasing and dodging skills, and encouraging their active involvement in the club.

Practice Sessions

Throughout the year, we conduct structured training and conditioning sessions aimed at enhancing speed, sprinting endurance, reflex times, and overall athletic performance on the field.

Practice Matches

We engage in friendly matches with neighbouring institutions to gain competitive exposure, refine defensive strategies, and prepare players for the dynamics of real game situations.

IISM (Inter IISER Sports Meet)

Our kho-kho team proudly represents IISER Kolkata at this prestigious national-level tournament, competing against teams from other IISERs.

Interbatch Matches

This intra-campus tournament pits different academic batches against each other, cultivating healthy competition, batch pride, and robust team camaraderie.

All-Star Matches

Exhibition games that showcase the exceptional talents of our best chasers and runners from various batches, celebrating individual prowess and strengthening our club's community bonds.

Auction Tournament

A unique, franchise-style tournament where team managers select players through a live auction, promising highly balanced teams and an intensely competitive experience.

Farewell Match

A special event dedicated to honouring our graduating students, acknowledging their commitment, leadership, and invaluable contributions to the kho-kho club.

Achievements

The KCIK men's squad clinched the Gold Medal at the Inter-IISER Sports Meet (IISM), breaking an 11-year drought for the men's team. KCIK also takes pride in its expanding national footprint, actively training and supporting both men's and women's contingents to participate in various national-level Kho-Kho events.
