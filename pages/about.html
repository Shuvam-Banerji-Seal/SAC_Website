About the SAC

The Student Activity Council (SAC) is the elected student body that coordinates cultural activity at IISER Kolkata. We work with the ten cultural clubs on campus to plan events, allocate resources, and represent the student community.

What we do

  • Coordinate the academic-year event calendar across clubs.
  • Manage budgets and shared resources for cultural activities.
  • Select and send delegations to the Inter-IISER Cultural Meet (IICM).
  • Run the annual election for office bearers across all clubs.

Clubs under SAC

The SAC oversees ten cultural clubs spanning performing arts, media, literary, and visual arts. Visit the Clubs page to see them all, or check the Gallery for photos from recent events.

Get in touch

Questions, ideas, or feedback? Reach out via your club's office bearers, or look out for SAC office hours during the semester.
