Music Club — IISER K

The Music Club of IISER Kolkata is a vibrant community of students brought together by a shared love for music. Based in the dedicated music room (Room 104), the club serves as a creative haven where musical enthusiasts can practise, collaborate, and express themselves freely.

From jamming sessions and band rehearsals to solo practice hours, the club provides students with a space to explore and hone their musical abilities. Whether you are a seasoned musician or someone just beginning your musical journey, the Music Club welcomes all with open arms.

The club actively participates in inter-IISER and inter-college festivals, bringing laurels to the institute through its performances. Throughout the year, the Music Club organizes a range of events that celebrate every genre and style of music, fostering a rich culture of musical expression on campus.

Events

The Music Club hosts a range of events throughout the academic year that celebrate diverse musical genres and provide students with platforms to showcase their talent.

Rampage — Battle of Bands

Rampage is the flagship Battle of Bands event of the Music Club, bringing together bands from across campuses to compete in an electrifying showcase of musical prowess. The event is known for its high energy, enthusiastic crowds, and outstanding performances spanning rock, metal, fusion, and more.

Jhankaar

Jhankaar celebrates the rich tradition of classical music at IISER Kolkata. The event provides a platform for students to perform and appreciate Indian classical music, including vocal and instrumental performances rooted in Hindustani and Carnatic traditions.

Voice — Singing Competition

Voice is a popular solo singing competition that draws participants from all batches of IISER Kolkata. The competition provides an open stage for singers of all genres and styles to showcase their vocal abilities and passion for music.
