Slashdot — Coding & Design Club

Slashdot — The Coding and Design Club of IISER Kolkata was conceptualized in 2019 and officially formed on January 8th, 2021 by dedicated members from the 16MS, 17MS, 18MS, and 19MS batches. The club creates a community where students can learn coding and graphic design while having fun. Open to everyone interested, regardless of prior experience.

What We Focus On

Slashdot's activities are organised around four broad themes:

  • Web Development
  • Data Science and AI/ML
  • Simulations and Scientific Visualization
  • Random Fun/Cool Stuff

Cool Stuff by Members

The WeLearn Bot

Built by Amarjeet Kumar (18MS), Parth Bibekar (19MS), Abhay Kshirsagar (19MS), and Satvik Saha (19MS).

The PhyXit Bot

A Discord bot for physics enthusiasts that can simulate physical systems, fetch ArXiv papers, summarise Wikipedia articles, and pull up a professor's Google Scholar profile. Built by Rohan Kumar (21MS), Sattwamo Ghosh (21MS), Apuroop Vaidyam (21MS), Priyanshu Mahato (21MS), and Tushar Gayan (19MS).

Zoop — Anonymous Sharing

An anonymous-sharing social platform built by Abhiroop Chauhan (19MS).

Ani CLI

A command-line tool for browsing and streaming anime, shared as a fun side-project within the community.
